## 4) Visual & Interaction Spec (Plain Language)

### Layout & spacing philosophy
- One-column layout, premium margins, disciplined line length.
- Whitespace as a design feature (calm, “executive energy”).
- Clear vertical rhythm between sections.

### Typography feel
- Modern, high-legibility typography.
- Strong hierarchy: name → title → highlights → body.
- Numeric highlights look like “investment-grade proof”.

### Color palette
- Background: warm white / soft neutral
- Text: deep charcoal
- Accent: muted cool tone (subtle blue / slate)
- Avoid loud colors and gradients.

### Buttons and link behavior
- Primary button: solid accent, understated.
- Secondary (LinkedIn): outline or minimal fill.
- Hover: subtle darken / underline; no playful bounce.

### Motion principles
- Smooth scroll.
- Gentle reveals (fade + small movement).
- No parallax, no heavy effects.
- Motion should feel like luxury UI: quiet and intentional.

### Mobile behavior
- Hero stacks cleanly.
- CTA buttons become full-width.
- Proof highlights become stacked cards.
- Ensure touch targets are comfortable and spacing stays calm.
